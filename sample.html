Free-Cycle this Monday!

Sustainability Calvin calvinsustainability@gmail.com

The 2013 Calvin Freecycle event is coming up on Earth Day, Monday, April 22nd, to encourage all Calvin students to "recycle" their old things so that other students can take them (for free!).

We will be collecting items you wish to donate from Thursday, April 18th until Monday, April 22nd; simply bring old clothing, books, pictures, and other accessories to the shopping cart that will be stationed in your dorm's main lobby.

Then, please join us on Commons Lawn from 12:00-4:00 PM on the 22nd to participate in the actual Freecycle event! There will be free cookies and tables full of the donated items that ALL Calvin students, both on-campus and off-campus, are free to take, regardless of whether or not you personally donated any items. In case of rain, the Freecycle will be held in the Hekman Library Lobby at the same scheduled time.

If you have any questions about this exciting event, please ask your dorm's Sustainability Coordinator(s). Don't forget to donate items beginning this Friday or to attend the event on Monday, the 22nd!

immigration reform. This week Calvin is joining G92, a Christian

the Republican Party for immigration reform, and please continue
